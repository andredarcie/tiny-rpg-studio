# Tiny RPG Studio - Features

## Sharing & Publishing
- Make your own top-down RPG game right in the browser, no downloads, no installs
- Share your game with a single URL, just send the link to your friends and they can play it instantly
- Export your game as a standalone HTML file that works offline
- Save your project as a JSON file and reload it later to keep working on it
- Install it as an app on your phone or desktop and use it offline

## World Building
- Your world has 9 rooms (3x3 grid), each room is its own area you can design freely
- A minimap shows which room you are in at all times
- Comes with 16 ready-to-use tiles to build your maps (grass, stone floors, water, lava, trees, walls, doors, and more)
- Paint your world room by room using a simple tile brush, just click and drag
- Some tiles animate on their own: water shimmers, lava bubbles, grass sways
- Moving between rooms plays a smooth sliding transition
- Full undo and redo so you can experiment freely without fear of messing things up

## Enemies & Combat
- Place up to 6 enemies per room, choose from 8 different types: Giant Rat, Bandit, Skeleton, Dark Knight, Necromancer, Dragon, Fallen King, and Ancient Demon
- Three of them are bosses with higher stats and tougher fights
- Each enemy has its own stats: different amounts of life, damage, and hit chance
- Enemies warn you before attacking with a red pulsing glow so you have a chance to dodge
- Damage numbers float off enemies when hit, and combat messages appear on screen ("Backstab!", "Miss!", "Too fast!")
- A satisfying death animation plays when you defeat an enemy
- The screen shakes when the player takes heavy damage
- Taking damage gives you a brief moment of invulnerability so you can escape

## Characters & Story
- Your game opens with a title screen showing your game name and your name as the author
- Place NPCs in your world and write custom dialog for each one: signs, villagers, kings, mages, elves, dwarves, and more (26 character types total)
- NPCs can say different things depending on what the player has done, so you can create branching stories
- Set a custom ending message that appears when the player reaches the end of your game
- The screen goes grayscale on game over for a dramatic effect

## Items & Interactivity
- Add keys, potions, swords, levers, doors, and XP scrolls to your rooms
- Picking up items shows a short animation on screen
- Connect levers to magical doors: flip a switch and unlock a passage somewhere else in the world
- Swords found in the world also block incoming damage, not just deal it
- Give players swords they can find: wooden, bronze, or steel, each with different damage and durability

## Player Progression
- Players level up to level 10 and pick one of 6 special skills
- Skill choices are presented as a pick-one-of-two screen so the player always has a meaningful decision
- Skills include: walk on lava, pick locks without keys, backstab enemies, brew stronger potions, revive once after dying, and charm NPCs for extra rewards

## Look & Feel
- Customize the look of your game with 9 built-in color palettes: PICO-8, Game Boy Green, Grayscale, NES, Commodore 64, and more
- Edit any palette color by hand to make the game look exactly the way you want
- Export your palette as a .gpl file to use in GIMP or other pixel art tools

## Testing
- Test your game at any time with a single click
- Start at a specific level or turn on god mode to explore without dying

## Mobile
- Works on mobile with a virtual D-pad included for touchscreen play
- The editor also works on mobile with a tab-based layout for each tool

## Language
- The editor is available in English and Portuguese
